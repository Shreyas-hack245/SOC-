What we simulated

Three real attack classes, launched from Kali Linux against our own hardened network, to prove the defenses actually hold up under fire.

RECON

Nmap port scans

Full TCP/UDP sweeps across the subnet to fingerprint open services before any exploit attempt — the same first move a real attacker makes.

Tool: nmapSource: Kali
DISRUPTION

SYN flood DoS

High-volume half-open TCP connections aimed at exhausting the target's connection table and taking services offline.

Tool: hping3Vector: TCP/SYN
INTRUSION

FTP / Telnet brute force

Automated credential-stuffing attempts against legacy plaintext services to test account lockout and detection thresholds.

Tool: HydraTarget: FTP · Telnet
